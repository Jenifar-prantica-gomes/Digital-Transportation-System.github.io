problem statement
1.Boss told Mr. Rahim to go Pabna for business purpose. He has never been there and also he has no idea. Then he decides that he will use public transport. But he doesn’t know where he should buy tickets. Also he has no time . He need the ticket immediately.
2.In train station we have to wait a lot. We have no idea when the train will arrive. It waste a lot of time and when its time to buy a ticket, we have to wait in a queue. There has no possibility weather I will get ticket or not. Sometimes we also face money transection problem. Will I get the desire sit or not! I wish we have digitalization on our public transportation.

Digital Transportation System

DUROTTO

Digital Bangladesh is one of the nation's dreams, and so special emphasis is given on the application of digital technologies to realise Vision 2021, which we commonly call Digital Bangladesh. Almost everything has digitalization in our country. That’s why we think about a project about public transportation which needs digitalization. The idea came from our real life problems.

Features

Features

Shows estimated price to get to the destination via bus.
Lets others follow your ride in real-time.
Easy reservation of bus seats on the go.
Shows hotspots from where local rickshaws and CNGs can be found.
Sync Reservations using Calendar Shortcuts.
Easy Payment Options.

Idea behind the project

No existing digital system for public transportation.
Developed countries have but not portable.
An app which could digitalized the public transportation

Goal

Our goal is to provide easy and secured access to public transports to distant/remote places in the country.
This could generally help the user to avoid the unorganized situation while getting a ticket when going to remote locations as well as ensuring a seat has been reserved.

third party services

Bikash & Nogod for transaction purposes.
Fast.
Secured.
Google for their Google map services.
Accurate.
Provide GPS services.

Database

We also use Firebase which is a real time database. The Firebase Real-time Database is a cloud-hosted database. We build an app with our iOS, Android, and JavaScript SDKs, all of our clients share one Real-time Database instance and automatically receive updates with the newest data.

Implementation

We will use open source resource.
We will develop the app by using JAVA, Android Studio.

About app

DUROTTO will show the price of different public transport. It will also show how many ways we can to go to our desire place. We can book a ticket easily. We can also select our desire sit. Most important we can know the bus starting time and that will save a lot of time. We can buy ticket through mobile banking because it is very easy to use and its safe.
